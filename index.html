Delhi, India

India is a large country in South Asia and the world’s most populous democracy. It has a rich history spanning thousands of years, being home to ancient civilizations like the Indus Valley. India is culturally diverse, with many languages, religions, and traditions. It gained independence from British rule in 1947 and today has a fast-growing economy, strong traditions in science and technology, and a vibrant cultural heritage.

Madrid, Spain

Madrid is the capital and largest city of Spain, located in the center of the country. It is known for its royal history, world-class museums like the Prado, lively nightlife, and vibrant culture. Madrid is also Spain’s political, economic, and cultural hub.

London, United Kindom

London is the capital city of the United Kingdom and one of the world’s most important global cities. It is known for its long history, iconic landmarks like Big Ben and Buckingham Palace, and cultural diversity. London is a major center for finance, politics, culture, and tourism.

Germany, Europe

Germany is a country in central Europe known for its strong economy, rich history, and cultural influence. It is a federal republic made up of 16 states, with Berlin as its capital and largest city. Germany is recognized for its contributions to science, engineering, philosophy, and the arts, as well as for its high-quality manufacturing, especially in the automotive and technology sectors.

Venis, Italy

The Amazon Rainforest is the largest tropical rainforest in the world, mainly located in South America. It is known for its vast biodiversity, home to millions of plant and animal species. The Amazon also plays a crucial role in regulating the Earth’s climate and producing oxygen.
